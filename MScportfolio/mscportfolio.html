A selection of work from my MSc Psychology

CBT and the neoliberal political agenda

A critical essay challenging the dominance of Cognitive Behavioural Therapies, considering the evidence base for their efficacy and the economic/political interests served by such dominance.

PTSD in displaced persons

A critical review of three recommended interventions for the relief of Post-traumatic Stress Disorder in refugees and asylum seekers.

Results section

A short sample results section, reporting a mixed 2x2 ANOVA focusing on preference for low-pitch in male and female voices.

Peer review

A sample peer review, discussing an investigation of the effect of concreteness vs. abstractness on recall of words.

Finger ratio, intelligence and study choice

A quantitative report of a secondary data analysis, considering finger ratio, the intelligence scores and university subject choice.

Is adults' social media use optimal in their own view?

A thematic analysis of data from a focus group discussing adults' use of social media and its effects on sleep.
